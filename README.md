# Trader Performance vs Market Sentiment Analysis

## Objective
This project analyzes the relationship between Bitcoin market sentiment (Fear/Greed) and trader behavior on Hyperliquid. The goal is to identify performance patterns and derive actionable trading insights.

## Key Insights
- Trader profitability is highest during Extreme Greed periods, supported by stronger win rates and lower volatility.
- Traders tend to increase position sizes during Fear phases, indicating elevated risk-taking behavior.
- Overall profitability is driven by a small subset of large trades, emphasizing the importance of risk management.

## Strategy Recommendations
- Increase trading exposure during bullish sentiment regimes.
- Reduce position sizes in fear-driven markets to control downside risk.
- Focus on disciplined risk management to capture high-impact trades while limiting losses.

## How to Run
Open the notebook in Google Colab or Jupyter and run all cells sequentially.
